Define the dimensions for your canvas

To begin; create your canvas. Make it long and high any number of pixels between 5 and 50!

PICK THE COLOR TO START WITH.

Choose your color to begin with painting. Your recent colors will be saved in swatches. All of that you can find in the color menu!

Let your creativity kick in!

To draw, simply drag your mouse in strokes. If you make a mistake; no fear - double click on a pixel, or stroke while holding right-mouse-button should do the trick!

You can adjust your canvas' grid size; change its background color and see it with or without grid lines.

SAVE YOUR MASTERPIECE!

If you like what you've created, save your masterpiece, so you can show it off. If not - oh well, just scrape it! And don't worry - artists are usually their own worst critics!
